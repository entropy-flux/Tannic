\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{classtannic_1_1operation_1_1Binary}{Binary} element-\/wise addition of two tensor expressions. 

Used to build a lazily evaluated {\ttfamily \doxylink{classtannic_1_1operation_1_1Binary}{Binary}\texorpdfstring{$<$}{<}\doxylink{structtannic_1_1operation_1_1Addition}{Addition}, Augend, Addend\texorpdfstring{$>$}{>}} expression. Computation is deferred until {\ttfamily \doxylink{structtannic_1_1operation_1_1Addition_aaf1e81033a9b9907db9f9c3c94b458f9}{forward()}} is called or assigned to a {\ttfamily \doxylink{classtannic_1_1Tensor}{Tensor}}.
