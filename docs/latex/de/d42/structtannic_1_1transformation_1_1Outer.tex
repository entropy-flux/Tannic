\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents the outer product operation between two vectors. 

The outer product of two vectors with shapes (n) and (m) results in a matrix of shape (n, m). Currently, only vectors are supported â€” tensors with rank greater than 1 are not supported, but this may be extended in the future.
