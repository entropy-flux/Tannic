\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Element-\/wise subtraction of two tensor expressions. 

Used to build a lazily evaluated {\ttfamily \doxylink{classtannic_1_1operation_1_1Binary}{Binary}\texorpdfstring{$<$}{<}\doxylink{structtannic_1_1operation_1_1Subtraction}{Subtraction}, Subtrahend, Minuend\texorpdfstring{$>$}{>}} expression. Computation is deferred until {\ttfamily \doxylink{structtannic_1_1operation_1_1Subtraction_afc988b4e53299042435409e149f961d3}{forward()}} is called or assigned to a {\ttfamily \doxylink{classtannic_1_1Tensor}{Tensor}}.
